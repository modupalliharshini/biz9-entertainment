The Un-Wedding Story

Bringing the magic of the 'Big Fat Indian Wedding' to young professionals.

The Event of the Year

Fake Wedding by Biz9 Entertainment is Hyderabad's first social celebration where people enjoy the grandeur of a wedding without any real bride or groom. It's a massive networking hub disguised as a festive celebration.

Whether it's the high-energy music, the gourmet buffet, or the chance to meet like-minded professionals in their best ethnic wear, Fake Wedding is where Hyderabad comes to celebrate life.

What is Fake Wedding?

A Fake Wedding is a professionally produced, ticketed wedding-style celebration.

  • 1 There is no real bride or groom
  • 2 Guests attend as wedding invitees
  • 3 The entire event is designed for entertainment, socializing, and celebration

Why Fake Wedding?

  • 1 High demand for experience-led nightlife
  • 2 Wedding aesthetics + party culture = strong youth pull
  • 3 Hyderabad lacks a mass wedding-party experience
